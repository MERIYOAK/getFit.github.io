Group Classes

Our gym offers a variety of group fitness classes that cater to different fitness levels and interests. Our experienced instructors will guide you through each class to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Aerobics

Our aerobics classes are designed to get your heart pumping and your body moving. We offer a range of aerobics classes, including high-impact and low-impact options, to suit your needs.

Yoga

Our yoga classes are a great way to improve your flexibility, balance, and mental clarity. We offer a range of yoga classes, including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Restorative, to suit your needs.

Zumba

Our Zumba classes are a fun and energetic way to get fit. Our certified instructors will guide you through each routine, which combines dance and fitness moves to help you burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health.

Cycling

Our cycling classes are a great way to get a cardio workout while improving your leg strength and endurance. Our bikes are adjustable to suit your needs, and our instructors will guide you through each class to help you achieve your goals.
